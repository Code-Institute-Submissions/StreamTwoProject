Overview

The information presented in the dashboard shows the current growth of the passenger traffic through Los Angeles Airport (LAX) over a specified time period. From the different graphs the makeup of the traffic can be broken down to different categories such as international and domestic as well as arrivals and departures. The graphs also give the opportunity to view the volume of traffic through each terminal giving indications of which terminals are being utilised or underutilised. Upon reviewing the data it is clear that the current trend of traffic is increasing which leads to the projection of continued increase over time. All the information then gives way to ideas of future proofing the airport in terms of terminal / runway expansions or possible additions.
